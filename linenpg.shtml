We bring you lovely textiles from the past! While some of our items have formal origins and some are "homespun country", each piece brings a special look for your home.

Our vintage pieces are acquired at auction, through estate sales and from private offerings. Many of the items come from northern Maryland and Southeastern Pennsylvania, including Lancaster County. Provenance is provided, if known.

We include care instructions with each item. All our linens are freshly washed and pressed, ready for your immediate enjoyment.

Browse through our "linen closet". We are careful to select items in nice condition. Please do keep in mind that these items are old and most have had at least one previous owner. We do our best to carefully describe each item. If you have any questions about any item, please email us.
