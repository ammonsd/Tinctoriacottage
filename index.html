Welcome to Tinctoria Cottage —
where we share with you our love of textiles. Enjoy the colors, textures and patterns of fabrics and threads, honoring the time when handwork was central to the home.

We carry a selection of items representing the needle arts — embroidery, crochet, knitting, weaving, fine hand sewing — from 'lace and linens' to 'rick rack and gingham'.

We hope that you'll find something to interest you here, in our carefully selected inventory of table and kitchen linens, hankies, doilies, bed linens and more.

If you are searching for a special item, please inquire. We may have just what you are looking for!
